Power Washing

Revitalize Your Property: Palma Cleaning's Premier Power Washing Services in Toronto

Welcome to Palma Cleaning, your trusted partner for professional power washing services in Toronto. Our dedicated team is committed to revitalizing your property's exterior surfaces, restoring their beauty and enhancing curb appeal. With state-of-the-art equipment and environmentally friendly cleaning solutions, we deliver exceptional results that exceed your expectations. Explore our comprehensive range of power washing services tailored to meet your specific needs.

Residential Power Washing

House Washing

Transform the appearance of your home with our comprehensive house washing services. We remove dirt, grime, mold, and mildew from all exterior surfaces, including siding, brick, stucco, and more, restoring your home's beauty and curb appeal.

Driveway and Walkway Cleaning:

Make a lasting impression with clean, well-maintained driveways and walkways. Our power washing experts utilize high-pressure cleaning techniques to remove stubborn stains, oil spills, and debris, leaving your surfaces looking like new.

Deck and Patio Cleaning

Extend your outdoor living space's lifespan with our professional deck and patio cleaning services. We effectively remove dirt, algae, moss, and other contaminants, revitalizing your outdoor entertainment areas and ensuring they're ready for relaxation and enjoyment.

Commercial Power Washing

Building Exterior Cleaning

Enhance your commercial property's curb appeal and attract customers with a pristine exterior. Our power washing specialists are equipped to tackle all types of surfaces, including concrete, brick, metal, and more, removing dirt, graffiti, and other unsightly blemishes.

Parking Lot Cleaning

Maintain a clean and safe environment for customers and employees with our parking lot cleaning services. We remove oil stains, gum residue, and other debris, improving the appearance and functionality of your parking facilities.

Graffiti Removal:

Combat vandalism and preserve your property's appearance with our prompt and efficient graffiti removal services. Our experienced technicians employ specialized techniques and eco-friendly cleaning agents to restore surfaces without causing damage.
