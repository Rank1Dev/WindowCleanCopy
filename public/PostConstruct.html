Post Construction

Construction Chaos to Pristine Perfection: Palma Cleaning's Post-Construction Clean Up Services in Toronto

Welcome to Palma Cleaning, your premier choice for post-construction clean-up services in Toronto. As the dust settles and the final touches are put in place, trust our experienced team to meticulously clean and prepare your construction site for its grand reveal. With attention to detail and a commitment to excellence, we ensure that your property shines, ready to impress clients and occupants alike. Explore our comprehensive range of post-construction clean-up services tailored to meet your specific needs.

Initial Clean-Up Phase

Debris Removal

Our skilled technicians swiftly and safely remove construction debris, including lumber, drywall scraps, packaging materials, and more, clearing the way for the next phase of your project.

Dust and Dirt Extraction

Using specialized equipment and techniques, we eliminate dust and dirt from every surface, including floors, walls, ceilings, and fixtures, ensuring a clean and pristine environment for final inspections and occupancy.

Surface Cleaning

We meticulously clean all surfaces, from countertops and cabinets to windows and trim, removing construction residue and preparing your space for finishing touches and furnishings.

Interior Detailing

Floor Care

Whether your floors are hardwood, tile, or carpet, we provide comprehensive cleaning and restoration services to remove construction-related stains, marks, and residue, leaving your floors gleaming and ready for foot traffic.

Kitchen and Bathroom Sanitization

We pay special attention to high-traffic areas such as kitchens and bathrooms, thoroughly sanitizing countertops, sinks, toilets, and other fixtures to ensure a clean and hygienic environment for occupants.

Interior Window Cleaning

Experience the clarity of crystal-clear windows with our interior window cleaning services. We remove dust, smudges, and other debris, allowing natural light to flood your space and showcase its beauty.

Exterior Beautification

Pressure Washing

Restore the exterior of your property to its former glory with our professional pressure washing services. We remove dirt, grime, and other unsightly buildup from surfaces such as siding, sidewalks, driveways, and parking lots, enhancing curb appeal and leaving a lasting impression.

Landscape Cleanup

We tidy up exterior spaces, removing construction-related debris and restoring landscaping elements to their pre-construction condition. From lawn maintenance to tree and shrub trimming, we ensure that your property's exterior looks pristine and inviting.

Final Touches

Our team performs a thorough walkthrough to address any remaining cleaning and touch-up tasks, ensuring that every detail is perfect before turning the keys over to you or your clients.

Specialized Services

Hazardous Material Removal

Safety is our top priority. We safely and responsibly remove hazardous materials such as lead paint, asbestos, and other contaminants, ensuring compliance with all regulatory standards and guidelines.

Air Duct Cleaning

Promote a healthy indoor environment with our professional air duct cleaning services. We remove dust, debris, and allergens from your HVAC system, improving air quality and reducing the risk of respiratory issues for occupants.

Green Cleaning Solutions

We prioritize environmental sustainability and offer eco-friendly cleaning solutions that are safe for your property and the planet. Our green cleaning practices minimize environmental impact while delivering exceptional results.
