Window Cleaning

Crystal Clear: Palma Cleaning's Comprehensive Window Cleaning Services in Toronto

Welcome to Palma Cleaning, your premier choice for window cleaning services in the bustling city of Toronto. With our commitment to excellence and attention to detail, we bring clarity and shine to both residential and commercial properties. Our skilled team utilizes state-of-the-art equipment and eco-friendly cleaning solutions to ensure your windows sparkle, enhancing the aesthetics and ambiance of your space. Explore our range of services tailored to meet your specific needs.

Residentail Window Cleaning:

Interior Window Cleaning:

Experience the joy of sunlight streaming through pristine windows with our interior cleaning services. We carefully remove dust, dirt, and smudges, leaving your windows spotless and streak-free.

Exterior Window Cleaning

Enhance your home's curb appeal with our professional exterior window cleaning. From ground-level bungalows to multi-story residences, our team tackles windows of all shapes and sizes, restoring their brilliance and clarity.

Screen and Sill Cleaning

Complete your window cleaning experience with our thorough screen and sill cleaning services. We pay attention to every detail, ensuring that even the often-overlooked areas are immaculately clean, allowing fresh air to flow freely into your home.

Commercial Window Cleaning

Storefront Window Cleaning

Attract attention and draw customers into your establishment with our storefront window cleaning services. We understand the importance of maintaining a pristine storefront, and our team works diligently to remove fingerprints, smudges, and other blemishes, ensuring that your windows showcase your products and services in the best possible light.

Office Building Window Cleaning

Create a bright, inviting workspace for your employees with our office building window cleaning services. Clean windows not only enhance natural light but also contribute to a positive work environment. Let us help you maintain a professional image with sparkling clean windows that leave a lasting impression.

Specialized Services: Going Beyond the Basics

Hard Water Stain Removal

Say goodbye to unsightly hard water stains with our specialized removal techniques. Our team effectively eliminates mineral deposits, restoring the clarity and brilliance of your windows without causing damage.
